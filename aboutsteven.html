Professional Summary

With a career rooted in finance and business strategy, Steven Kay brings over two decades of multifaceted experience across reporting and analytics, sales, training, credit underwriting, management, and project leadership. A dynamic and results-oriented professional, Steven has consistently led initiatives that blend innovation, efficiency, and purpose—driving measurable improvement in business operations while inspiring and developing the people around him.

Steven's strength lies not just in data and decision-making but in empowering others. From managing high-impact projects and training programs to building strategic communication platforms and leading process optimization, his focus remains steadfast: creating systems that help others grow, thrive, and succeed.

Steven is deeply committed to community leadership and volunteerism, driven by a genuine desire to serve and uplift others. This passion is what inspired the creation of this site—an extension of his belief that professional tools and skills should be accessible, practical, and empowering for everyone.

With a solid foundation in organizational leadership and technical skills spanning Excel, SQL, Python, Tableau, Power BI, and SharePoint, Steven blends analytical expertise with human-centered impact. Whether it's designing a training module or crafting an executive report, his approach is clear: lead with purpose, execute with precision, and always focus on making a difference.
